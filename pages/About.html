About HG Logistics

HG Logistics is a Pakistan-based Freight and Warehousing company specializing in Truckload (TL) and Less than Truckload (LTL) shipping services.
We have served the local and international community for over 50 years by providing carrier service between all over the world. Today, HG Logistics has grown into one of the top trucking and transportation companies in Pakistan, providing freight & logistics services in Asia, Gulf, Europe and US. We also offer nationwide and international coverage through our partner carriers. Please make sure to check out our careers page for open positions and benefit information. We are currently hiring for a Class A CDL driver!

With a combined experience of over 125 years in the trucking industry, our experts will provide you with the peace of mind you deserve. Whether you need logistic & transport services, warehousing fulfilment, or anything in-between, we are committed to meeting all of your freight & warehousing needs with professionalism and dependability. At HG Logistics 12-acre facility, we have over 100 owned equipment units in our fleet ready to handle your freight. Our climate-controlled and racked warehouse will keep your goods safe and organized for as long as you need. HG Logistics guarantees we’ll be the last RI transportation company you’ll ever need.
